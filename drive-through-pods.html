Drive Through Pods

Premium roadside pod accommodations engineered for national highways, expressways, and long-distance travel corridors.

Overview

Redefining Roadside Luxury

Drive Through Pods capture the underserved market of India's 150,000+ km national highway network. Positioned strategically at high-traffic junctions, these pods offer road travellers a safe, clean, and premium rest option — turning highway pitstops into revenue-generating luxury assets.

  • Drive-in accessibility with parking
  • 24/7 automated check-in system
  • EV charging point integration
  • Complimentary food and beverage zone
  • Baggage and vehicle security
  • High visibility branding for highway marketing

India's NH network carries millions of travellers daily. Premium highway pods capture this volume and convert it to consistent luxury revenue.

FAQ

Common Questions

What highway permissions are required? +
Site-specific permissions vary by state and highway authority (NHAI, state PWD, etc.). Our team assists franchise partners through the full regulatory process, including site selection that aligns with NHAI wayside amenity policy guidelines.
What is the land area requirement? +
A minimum viable drive-through pod cluster of 10 pods, including parking, F&B zone, and access road, requires approximately 0.75–1.5 acres. Larger configurations scale accordingly.
How is revenue generated in highway locations? +
Revenue comes from pod bookings (hourly and overnight), EV charging fees, F&B services, and optional retail partnerships. Highway pods typically achieve 60–75% occupancy during peak travel seasons.
