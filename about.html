Our Story

Who We Are

A Studio Born from
Passion & Purpose

Studio 361° Architects was founded in 2015 with one unwavering belief — that architecture is more than construction. It is the art of shaping human experience. Every space we design carries the weight of that conviction.

Based in Salem, our multidisciplinary team of architects, interior designers and visualization specialists collaborate across every phase — from the first sketch to the final key handover. The result is spaces that are as meaningful as they are beautiful.

Our Approach

Design Philosophy

Three principles guide every decision we make — from the broadest urban gesture to the smallest material detail.

Form Follows Purpose

Every spatial decision begins with a deep understanding of how people will inhabit the space — function is never compromised for aesthetics, both must coexist seamlessly.

Contextual Sensitivity

We believe architecture must respond to its environment — climate, culture, community and landscape all inform every design decision we make.

Material Integrity

We champion honest materials — timber, stone, concrete and steel used truthfully, celebrating their natural qualities rather than disguising them behind superficial finishes.

Our Journey

A Decade of Design

2015

Studio Founded

Studio 361° Architects was established in Salem with a team of three, driven by a vision to redefine premium architecture in South India.

2017

First Major Villa Project

Completion of the award-winning Moorthy Residence in Adyar, establishing Studio 361° as a name synonymous with luxury residential design.

2019

3D Visualization Wing Launched

We expanded our services with a dedicated 3D visualization department, allowing clients to experience their spaces before construction begins.

2021

Best Design Award

Honoured with the Indian Architecture & Design Excellence Award for the Coimbatore Commercial Complex — our first major commercial commission.

2024

200+ Projects Milestone

We celebrated the completion of our 200th project — a luxury beachfront villa in ECR — marking a decade of transformative design across India.
