Who We Are

POD STAYS — Luxury Redefined

POD STAYS is India's first organized luxury pod resort chain — a brand born from the intersection of German engineering precision, eco-conscious design philosophy, and a deep understanding of modern experiential travel.

With over 30 years of expertise in advanced modular construction, our team has pioneered a hospitality model that makes luxury accessible, scalable, and sustainable — all at once.

Our Journey

A Decade of Building Tomorrow

From a vision to redefine Indian hospitality to becoming the category creator for luxury pod resorts — our journey has been marked by relentless innovation.

Foundations

30+ Years in Modular Construction

The founding team establishes deep expertise in advanced, eco-friendly modular building using German CNC technology — laying the groundwork for what would become POD STAYS.

The Idea

POD STAYS Concept Born

Recognizing a massive gap in India's experiential hospitality market, the concept for India's first organized luxury pod resort chain takes shape.

2026 — Now

Yercaud Flagship Launch

POD STAYS's first resort at Yercaud, Tamil Nadu launches with 9+ pod models, premium amenities, and the FOCO franchise model. The category is created.

2027 → Beyond

Pan-India & Global Expansion

Active pipeline across Tamil Nadu, Kerala, Himachal Pradesh — followed by UAE, Thailand, Bali and Sri Lanka. 500+ pods by 2029.
