The OHC Story

Born from a Belief That Prevention Is Paramount

OHC Services was established with a singular purpose: to transform preventive healthcare from a reactive afterthought into a proactive, seamlessly integrated part of professional life. Our founder, Dr. G. Karthick — MBBS, DFM, FICM, C.DIAB — identified a critical gap. Executives and corporate professionals, the very people driving India's economy, were the least likely to prioritize their own health until crisis demanded it.

Dr. Karthick's response was to eliminate every friction point between busy professionals and quality healthcare. By bringing the clinic to the client — via mobile units, workplace camps, and home collection services — OHC reimagined what preventive medicine could look like in the modern era.

Today, with NABL accreditation, ISO certification, QCI membership, and an 80-strong corporate client roster spanning Tamil Nadu's most recognized organizations, OHC Services stands as the definitive leader in executive and corporate healthcare.

Our Journey

Key Milestones in Clinical Excellence

From our founding to achieving national accreditation, each milestone represents a deeper commitment to the people and organizations who rely on us.

Foundation
OHC Services Established

Dr. G. Karthick founds OHC Services with a mission to bring preventive healthcare to executives and corporates across Tamil Nadu, pioneering the mobile healthcare model in the region.

Growth Phase
Corporate Expansion

Onboarding of anchor corporate clients including MRF, CUMI, and Murugappa Group. Establishment of systematic onsite health camp protocols for large-scale workforce health management.

2017
AERB Licensing

Obtained Atomic Energy Regulatory Board acknowledgment for portable X-ray operations, enabling full radiography services within mobile health units — a significant capability milestone.

2019
Bio-Medical Waste Authorization

Received Tamil Nadu Pollution Control Board authorization for compliant bio-medical waste management, reinforcing OHC's commitment to environmental and regulatory responsibility.

2020
QCI Organisational Membership

Admitted as an Organisational Member of the Quality Council of India — India's apex body for quality promotion — affirming OHC's alignment with national quality standards.

2024
NABL Accreditation

Awarded NABL Medical (Entry Level) Testing Lab accreditation (NABL-M(EL)T-02461), valid through January 2027 — the culmination of our quality journey and a defining mark of clinical credibility.

Accreditations & Certifications

Standards That Speak for Themselves

Our certifications are not decorative — they are independently verified proof of the clinical and operational standards our team upholds daily.

NABL Accreditation

NABL-M(EL)T-02461 — Medical (Entry Level) Testing Lab certification from the National Accreditation Board for Testing and Calibration Laboratories. Valid through January 2027.

ISO 9001:2008

International quality management system certification recognizing OHC's rigorous process controls, continual improvement practices, and commitment to client satisfaction.

QCI Membership

Organisational Member of the Quality Council of India (Member No. ODRP/Lab/6847/2020-21) — India's apex body for quality promotion under the National Board for Quality Promotion.

EQAS Enrollment

Enrolled in the External Quality Assurance Scheme program (ISHTM-AIIMS, QAS-BC-Entry Level-00031) recognized by WHO and conducted through CMC Vellore and BIORAD.

AERB Licensing

Atomic Energy Regulatory Board acknowledgment (Case No. TN-47624-RF-XR-001) for portable radiography equipment, ensuring safe and compliant X-ray operations across all service locations.

Bio-Medical Authorization

Tamil Nadu Pollution Control Board authorization (No. 19BAD26784391) for responsible bio-medical waste generation, collection, storage, and disposal — meeting all statutory environmental requirements.
