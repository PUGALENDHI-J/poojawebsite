Who We Are

Handcrafted Herbal
Beauty Since 2020

Sparkle Shine was born from a simple belief: that the most powerful skincare comes from nature. Founded by a passionate herbalist in Tamil Nadu, every product is crafted from hand-selected botanical ingredients that have nourished Indian skin for generations.

We blend ancient Ayurvedic wisdom with modern formulation science, creating products that are not only effective but entirely free from harmful chemicals. Every jar, every bottle, every pack — made with love and integrity.

Our Journey

How We Grew

🌱

The Beginning

Growing Roots
