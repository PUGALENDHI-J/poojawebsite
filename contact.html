Frequently Asked Questions

Are Sparkle Shine products 100% natural?
+

Yes, absolutely. Every Sparkle Shine product is formulated with only natural, plant-based ingredients. We use no parabens, sulphates, artificial fragrances, or harmful chemicals. Our ingredient list is fully transparent.

Do you offer free shipping?
+

Yes! We offer free shipping across India on all orders. Delivery typically takes 3–7 business days depending on your location. For expedited delivery, please contact us directly.

Are the products safe for sensitive skin?
+

Our formulations are gentle and safe for all skin types, including sensitive skin and children's skin. However, as with any new product, we recommend doing a small patch test on your inner arm before full application.

How do I place an order?
+

The easiest way is to WhatsApp us at 7338801545 with the products you want. You can also call us at 7603967175 or fill in the contact form above. We'll confirm your order and arrange delivery.

What is the shelf life of your products?
+

Most of our products have a shelf life of 6–12 months from the date of manufacture. Exact shelf life details are listed on each product's packaging. Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.

Do you offer wholesale or bulk pricing?
+

Yes! We offer wholesale pricing for salons, resellers, and bulk orders. Please contact us via phone or WhatsApp for wholesale enquiries and pricing. We'd love to partner with you.

Can I return or exchange a product?
+

We stand behind the quality of all our products. If you receive a damaged or defective product, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with a photo, and we'll arrange a replacement or refund.

Do you make custom or personalised products?
+

We offer a wide range of products that cater to most skin and hair needs. For specific concerns or custom formulations for bridal packages or salons, please reach out to us directly to discuss requirements.
