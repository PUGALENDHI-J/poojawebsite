Estate Plan
Master Planning

Mr. Arun Estate Plan, Yercaud

A thoughtfully designed private estate layout for Mr. Arun at Yercaud, optimising the undulating terrain through careful elevation planning. The scheme features gated entry, internal vehicular loops, structured planting zones and a central open lawn — balancing privacy with a sense of openness unique to hill-country living.

  • ✦ Terrain-sensitive site organisation
  • ✦ Gated security & entry sequencing
  • ✦ Landscape zoning with native species
  • ✦ Utility & drainage masterplan

Contour Analysis
Master Planning

Contour Site Analysis, Yercaud

Detailed topographic contour study for the Arun estate site at Yercaud, essential for informed cut-and-fill decisions, retaining wall placement and drainage engineering. This technical master document ensures every element of the estate is built in harmony with the natural gradient — preventing erosion while maximising usable flat area.

  • ✦ High-precision contour mapping
  • ✦ Cut & fill volume optimisation
  • ✦ Slope stabilisation strategy
  • ✦ Natural drainage channel alignment

Rain Trees
Master Planning

Rain Trees, Salem

A vision for sustainable urban living, Rain Trees is a master-planned community that prioritizes the preservation of mature landscape elements. The design integrates premium residential plots with lush green corridors, creating a seamless transition between the built environment and nature. The layout is optimized for natural ventilation, sunlight, and efficient water management.

  • ✦ Ecological preservation strategy
  • ✦ Solar-optimized site orientation
  • ✦ Advanced rainwater harvesting network
  • ✦ Native landscape restoration
