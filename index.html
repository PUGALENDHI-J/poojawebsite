Who We Are

Redefining the Standard of Preventive Medicine

OHC Services was founded on a singular conviction: that quality healthcare should fit seamlessly into the lives of the people who need it most. Under the leadership of Dr. G. Karthick — MBBS, DFM, FICM, C.DIAB — we have built Tamil Nadu's most trusted mobile and onsite healthcare institution.

From NABL-accredited diagnostic laboratories to fully equipped mobile health units, our comprehensive ecosystem brings world-class preventive care to corporate campuses, homes, and our own premium centers across Chennai.

Quality Assurance

Certified Accuracy You Can Trust
