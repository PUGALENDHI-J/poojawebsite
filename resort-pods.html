FAQ

Common Questions

What are the available resort pod configurations? +
We offer 6 resort pod configurations: Classic (150 sqft), Deluxe (200 sqft), Suite (280 sqft), Villa Pod (350 sqft), Presidential (400 sqft), and the Tunnel Pod with panoramic curved glazing. Each can be customized for your site.
What is the minimum land area required for a resort? +
A minimum cluster of 8 pods requires approximately 1 acre of land, inclusive of common areas, parking, and landscaping. Larger resorts of 20–50 pods typically require 3–8 acres depending on pod spacing and terrain.
How does the FOCO model work for resort pods? +
Under the FOCO (Franchise Owned, Company Operated) model, the investor owns the pod infrastructure and land, while POD STAYS operates the resort under our brand, handles bookings, marketing, and daily management. Revenue is shared per agreed terms with guaranteed minimum returns.
