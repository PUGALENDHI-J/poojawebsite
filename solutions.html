Corporate Solutions

End-to-End Workforce Health Management

OHC delivers a fully integrated suite of corporate health solutions — from onsite camps and pre-employment evaluations to ongoing health monitoring and digital record management — all customizable to your organization's needs.

Onsite Health Checkups

Fully equipped mobile medical units come directly to your premises. Zero commute for your employees, zero disruption to your operations. We handle everything from setup to report delivery.

  • Flexible scheduling — half-day or full-day camps
  • All diagnostic equipment brought onsite
  • Specialist panel available for consultation
  • Same-day preliminary reports available

Health Profiling & Analytics

Comprehensive individual and group health profiles with statistical trend analysis. HR administrators receive anonymized population-level insights to drive wellness program decisions.

  • Individual health risk scores
  • Department-level wellness dashboards
  • Year-on-year comparison reports
  • Dedicated HR portal with secure access

Pre-Employment Evaluation

Standardized, medico-legally sound pre-employment health assessments that align with your industry's regulatory requirements. Rapid turnaround ensures your hiring process remains uninterrupted.

  • Customizable to role-specific requirements
  • Digitally signed reports within 48 hours
  • Bulk processing for large hiring batches
  • Integrated with HR onboarding workflows

Periodical Health Statistics

Mandated periodic health examinations conducted at intervals specified by labour law and industry guidelines. Complete documentation and compliance reporting provided for regulatory submissions.

  • Statutory compliance for factories & industries
  • Annual, biannual, and custom schedules
  • Compliance certificates and audit documentation
  • Specialist referral pathways for flagged cases

Onsite Health Centre Management

Turn-key management of your dedicated onsite medical room or health centre. OHC provides trained medical staff, equipment maintenance, consumable supply, and reporting infrastructure.

  • Resident nurse / doctor placement
  • First aid and emergency response readiness
  • Monthly operational reports to management
  • Seamless integration with OHC digital platform

Home Collection Facility

Certified phlebotomists and technicians visit the patient's home for sample collection and basic screening. Ideal for senior executives, differently-abled employees, and post-treatment monitoring.

  • Early morning slots available (6 AM onwards)
  • Blood, urine, and ECG at home
  • Reports uploaded to patient portal within 24 hours
  • Nurse-led consultation available on request
Digital Infrastructure

Your Health Records,
Accessible Everywhere

OHC's Laboratory Information System (LIS), available at lis.ohcservices.com, provides secure, cloud-based access to every test result, historical comparison, and health trend — anytime, anywhere.

Whether you're reviewing last year's lipid panel or your HR team is generating a population wellness report for the board, the OHC platform delivers the data you need with the security and reliability it demands.

Encrypted Cloud Storage

All health records stored with bank-grade encryption. Your data is yours — private, protected, permanent.

Automated Report Delivery

Reports automatically emailed upon authorization. No chasing, no delays — just clarity when it matters.

Trend Comparison & Analytics

Compare specific tests across visits. See your health trajectory clearly with data-driven visual reporting.

Dual-Access HR & Individual Portals

Employees access personal records privately. HR receives population-level anonymized data for policy decisions.
