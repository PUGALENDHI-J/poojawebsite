Common Questions

A standard executive health checkup typically takes between 2.5 to 4 hours, depending on the package selected. This includes sample collection, all diagnostic tests, physician consultation, and preliminary results discussion. We recommend arriving fasting (no food for 8 hours prior) for accurate metabolic and blood sugar panels.
Yes. OHC specializes in onsite corporate health camps and can deploy our mobile diagnostic unit directly to your premises. We bring our laboratory equipment, ECG and echo machines, portable X-ray, and medical staff with us. You simply need to provide a suitable space (a large conference room is typically adequate) and we handle the rest.
Routine blood and urine reports are typically ready within the same working day. Specialized tests such as culture studies or advanced cardiac panels may take 24–72 hours. All reports are automatically uploaded to our secure online portal (lis.ohcservices.com) and emailed to you upon authorization by our pathologist.
Yes, we offer home collection for blood samples, urine samples, and ECG. Our certified phlebotomists can visit from 6:00 AM onwards. For more comprehensive home visits including physician consultation and portable diagnostic equipment, please contact us to arrange a custom visit. Home visits are available across Chennai.
OHC is NABL-accredited (Certificate No. NABL-M(EL)T-02461, valid through January 2027), ISO 9001:2008 certified, and enrolled in the WHO-recognized EQAS program conducted through CMC Vellore and BIORAD. We use fully computerized laboratory systems and independent third-party quality controls to ensure accuracy and reliability on every report we issue.
We can accommodate groups from as small as 10 employees upwards. For organizations with fewer than 10 participants, we recommend booking individual appointments at one of our centres. For groups of 50 or more, we can arrange a full-day dedicated camp with a customized test panel and on-the-spot consultation with our panel specialists.
